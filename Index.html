Frequently Asked Questions

The Monkey campervans are equipped with basic cooking equipment which includes cutlery, crockery, pots and pans, gas kettle, cooler box, sink, manual water pump and a little more. A Linen Pack can be purchased for $xxxx and includes a queen duvet, queen sheet, 2 towels, 2 pillow cases and a tea towel. Please note that if no Linen Pack is selected the vehicle comes with a duvet inner (or a sleeping bag) and 2 pillows complementary. If the sleeping bag is used it must be returned clean.
The Chimpanzee campervan have automatic transmissions making it easier to drive around Chile and the Gorilla 4X4 campervan are mechanic transmission.
The Monkey campervans has a 5 liters water tank. The fresh water tank can be easily refilled whenever you want at holiday parks and often a service station when you refuel the vehicle or any other tap.
In Chile you can camp for free in almost everywhere, because till there is not rule about it (just in a couple of beaches). Even camping in a Caravan Park is usually not that expensive (except for a few of the very remote communities!). Many of the beaches, mountains, lakes, desert in Chile allow camping, meaning you can follow the geography around Chile whilst saving your money and waking up each morning to the best view in the world!
We always suggest as soon as you know the dates you wish to have your holiday – go online and book. To confirm we need 50% deposit and then the balance is due on pick up of your campervan. Very easy. The best way to submit your credit card details are though the secure server bookings page on our website.
The campervan operators charge by the day, not per 24 hour period. So regardless of the time of collection and return, you will be charged for the day you collect and the day you drop your vehicle off. This is somewhat like a hotel and a rental car company all in one as you can drive and sleep in your vehicle.
Book your car, all you need is a credit or debit card. When you pick the car up, you'll need:
  • Your rentalcars.com voucher / eVoucher, to show that you've paid for the car
  • The main driver's credit / debit card, with enough available funds for the car's security deposit
  • Each driver's full, valid driving licence, which they've held for at least 12 months (often 24)
  • Your passport and any other ID the car hire company needs to see
Your request will be processed within 24 hours of receiving your booking or we will get in contact with you. If a vehicle is available for the dates you specified, your booking will be finalised and your credit card will be charged a 50% deposit. If a vehicle is not available, we will contact you with a suitable alternative and no payment will be incurred.
We require a credit card for the 50% deposit to secure you booking. Your credit card information is strictly confidential and this is a faster method for securing your booking compared with Cash or Cheque.
We accept Visa, Master Card, and American Express. Please note that have an additional fee of 3%. There is no extra charge for paying in cash.
All credit card transactions will be processed in Chilean pesos (CLP). This applies to all bookings made through our website.
If you wish to amend or cancel your existing booking, please contact us as soon as possible. This is so we can make sure any extensions are available for you and to reduce the cost to you for cancellations. Simply go to the contact page and make an enquiry, or email info@monkey-adventures.com. Please ensure you quote your confirmation number.

No, our campervans can be driven with a standard full car license. We recommend a Spanish translation or an international drivers license.
Drivers must hold a current and valid drivers license (non-probationary) for the duration of the hire period. Foreigners should use a foreign license, with the Spanish translation of its text. No minimum age restriction applies for 2wd vehicles and for the 4WD rentals which require minimum 22 years old.
We have a minimum hire period of 7 days when collecting and returning the campervan. Please note however that this can change during peak seasons depending on availability.
The open road speed limit is 120km/hour but we suggest a comfortable speed for driving your campervan is 90-100km/hour, especially if you have never driven one before. Should you choose to travel below the speed limit, please show courtesy to other road users by allowing them to pass.
This all depends on how far you travel! Most of our campervans have an average fuel consumption of 10-14 ltrs per 100kms.

One-Way rentals are available only with pick-up in Santiago and in coordination with the Monkey Adventure before of pick-up de car. (applies extra cost depending of the city).
Our campervans are supported 24-hour emergency breakdown service who are a nationwide network of reputable service agents who will deal with any problems that may arise. We can also authorize you to spend up to CLP$50.000 in emergency repairs so that you can deal with minor matters speedily or when convenient to you, but please produce the receipt when you return from your hire for a refund.
The rental insurance cover restricts the maximum you are liable for in the event of an accident as long as you do not break the rental agreement terms and conditions. It does not cover you for any personal loss or injury. We suggest taking your own personal travel insurance.
You will be liable for up to the amount of your excess towards the repair cost. If the damage is minor, you will only be charged the repair costs and not the entire excess.
If you are covered by travel insurance you will not need to pay for any additional insurance excess reduction, however you will be liable to pay the excess at the time of the incident and then you would need to reclaim this excess from your travel insurer.
In the event that something happens to the campervan and you are not at fault, (either a vehicle accident where someone strikes the vehicle or damage occurs while the vehicle is parked) you are liable for the damage caused to the vehicle until we are able to receive payment for those damages from the third party.
